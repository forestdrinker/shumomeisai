# 1. Symbol Dictionary & Logic (符号字典与逻辑库)

## Symbol Dictionary

- $s$: 赛季索引 (Season index), $s=1,\dots,34$
- $t$: 周索引 (Week index), $t=1,\dots,T_s$
- $i$: 选手索引 (Contestant index, celebrity-partner pair)
- $s_{i,t,j}$: 第 $t$ 周第 $j$ 位评委给选手 $i$ 的分数 (可缺失)
- $S_{i,t}$: 评委总分 (Total judges' score)
- $\mathcal{A}_{s,t}$: 当周仍在赛选手集合 (Active contestants set)
- $\mathcal{E}_{s,t}$: 当周淘汰集合 (Eliminated set, can be empty or multiple)
- $u_{i,t}$: 人气潜变量 (Latent popularity variable)
- $v_{i,t}$: 票份额 (Fan vote share, 观众票占比)
- $m_s$: 规则类型 (Rule type: rank/percent)
- $\text{save}_s$: 是否启用评委拯救规则 (Indicator for Judges’ Save)
- $\lambda$: 淘汰似然“尖锐度”参数 (Likelihood sharpness parameter)
- $\sigma_u^2$: 人气周际波动强度 (Week-to-week popularity volatility)
- $\kappa_J, \kappa_F$: Soft-rank 的温度参数 (Temperature parameters for soft-rank)
- $\gamma$: Judges’ save 中评委偏好强度参数 (Judge preference strength in save)
